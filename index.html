My Profile

I am a postdoc researcher at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology. I am part of the CMS collaboration at CERN, where my research line is centered around the CMS experiment at the LHC. My main focus is on the study of the Higgs boson and the reconstruction and calibration of muons. I try to get the best out of the available data using machine learning, keeping a tight bond with the physics target.

Projects

As a member of the CMS collaboration, large part of my work consists of the analysis of the data produced by the LHC. I also participate in the Neural-based diffusion likelihood-free estimations (NEEDLE) project, and in studying the prospects for searching for axion-like particles with the LUXE experiment at DESY. If you want to know more, you can check a selection of the theses I supervised and the topics I would like to investigate.
