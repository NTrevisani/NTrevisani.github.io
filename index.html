My Profile

I am a postdoc researcher at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology. I am part of the CMS collaboration at CERN, where I study the Higgs boson via its decay into pairs of W bosons and I work on the identification of muons in the detector. I also participate in the Neural-based diffusion likelihood-free estimations (NEEDLE) project, and in the search for axion-like particles with the LUXE experiment.
